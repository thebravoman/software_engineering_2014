The Greek military junta of 1967–74, commonly known as the Regime of the Colonels (καθεστώς των Συνταγματαρχών), or in Greece simply The Junta, The Dictatorship (Η Δικτατορία) and The Seven Years (Η Επταετία), was a series of right-wing military juntas that ruled Greece. Not few soldiers took part in the battles.
